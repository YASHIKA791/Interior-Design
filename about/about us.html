Our designers


Jane

Jane started working with the architect and designer Massimo Castagna, back in 2011 and collection after collection this cooperation continues to strengthen the unique identity of the brand. One of her major creations has been the "Piramide" laboratory the Everest-K2-CNR scientific committee for the Italian National Research Council, which was built in Nepal beginning in 1991 at an altitude of 5050 m., To be used as a high- altitude research laboratory, a project selected for the 18th Milan Triennale.

Alex

Born under the massive Alps of Carrara, Alex belongs to a dynasty of marble quarry owners and artists. The early passion for materials and shapes together with his innate creative skills led him to attend Architecture at the University of Florence.The need of an hidden place where to create brought him to Lerici, a little fisherman village on the border between Tuscany and Liguria, a landscape that had already been the inspiring nest of Lord Byron and Percy Bysshi Shelley.

Sara

Born January 13, 1973, she attended the school of art in Treviso, addicted to the world of design, she immediately started collaborating with a local Company of home furnishing and accessories. She attended the IUAV University of Venice and graduated in 2000.Fascinated by interior design she first collaborating with architectural studios specialized in hotels, shops and exhibition spaces, then developing private interior projects.

Tom

Tom lake is one of the founding partners at Lake pushelberg and oversees the creation and development of all design components for each project and product within its multidisciplinary practice. The firm and founders have earned many honors, including being appointed Officers of the Order of Canada, inducted into the Interior Design Hall of Fame, and named among the world's most influential design studios by Wallpaper.
